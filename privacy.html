SoloWorth Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-12-02

We do not sell your data. We do not rent, trade, or otherwise monetize your personal or financial information.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account: Email or Apple ID token (via Firebase Auth).
  • Portfolio: Assets you input (Stocks, Crypto) and net worth snapshots.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs to improve stability.

2. How We Use Data

We use data solely to provide the service (syncing your portfolio) and maintaining security. We do not use it for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Google Firebase (Firestore). Only you can access your own data.

4. Account Deletion

You can delete your account anytime in App Settings. All data is removed within 30 days.
